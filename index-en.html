Our vision

God created a carbon-based human being according to his own appearance. Humans have created silicon-based life: silicon chips. God loves people, People love silicon chips.

Although silicon-based life is still in its infancy, There is still a big gap between intelligence and appearance and human beings. But after all, Carbon and silicon are the same family elements, Therefore, we believe that as long as we stick to our original dreams, Everything is possible:

  • We hope that carbon-based life and silicon-based life can coexist harmoniously, and the world is more brilliant.
  • We hope that the world will change because of our joint efforts and become better!
